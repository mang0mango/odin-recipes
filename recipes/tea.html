Tea

This is how I make my tea.

Ingredients

  • 1 decaf teabag
  • 1 cup of boiling water
  • 80ml Oatly Barista

Instructions

  1. Put the teabag in a mug.
  2. Add the water as soon as it boils and let it brew for 3-5mins.
  3. Remove the teabag, squeezing firmly.
  4. Add the Oatly Barista to the mug.
