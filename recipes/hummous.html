Hummous

This is my personally perfected recipes, based originally on that of Yotam Ottolenghi but iterated over through many shortages of ingredients and time.

Ingredients

  • 120g tahini
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 66ml water
  • 1 can of chickpeas
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • Half tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Put the tahini and lemon juice in a bowl and mix together.
  2. Add the water and mix thoroughly until smooth.
  3. Add the can of chickpeas, cumin and salt and blend thoroughly with a hand blender until smooth.
