Brownie

This recipe is the best in the world, created by James Morton. I have made a single change, which is substituting caster sugar for light brown.

Ingredients

  • 250g dark chocolate
  • 250g ssalted butter
  • 3 eggs and 1 egg yolk
  • 300g light brown sugar
  • 60g white flour
  • 60g cocoa

Instructions

  1. Melt the chocolate and butter in the microwave.
  2. Gently mix together the eggs and sugar.
  3. Add the melted chocolate-butter mixture and mix gently.
  4. Seive in the flour and cocoa and mix gently.
  5. Bake at 160 degrees for about 40mins.
